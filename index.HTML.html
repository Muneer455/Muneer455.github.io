ECOJOZI

Our Mission

Ecoozi is a gamified simulation where players take on the role of developers building the Ecoozi app - an initiative tackling Johannesburg's waste problem through sustainable waste management and community engagement.

EcoJozi blends education, technology, and community action, turning waste into a resource and empowering players to build a cleaner, more sustainable Johannesburg!
